Wanna feel the joys and dispaires of trading money at the stock market, but don't want to lose your actual money?

Then you've come to the right place!

[Broker]

How does it work?

The game is played in the "Broker" tab. Once you navigated there, the game(-clock) starts. Here you can see your "Cash Balance" which is set to 50k â‚¬ at the beginning of the round, as well as a "Holding" and a "Profit" Card.

The Cash balance resambles your bank account. The Holding card displays the amount of stocks you're currently holding and how much those are worth in total. The profit displays how much profit you made since the beginning of the game.

Each second the stock value changes with a 50/50 chance by a value of +/- 1to5

Below the Stock diagram you have the option to enter an amount. This amount is the value of how many stocks you want to buy or sell for the value the stock has at time moment you press buy/sell.

The game ends once 10 minutes have passed or you decide to end the game by clicking "End Game"

Special features

•     Bullmarket: If the stock rises three times in a row the market is in a "Bullmarket" state. → The chances the stock rises again increases to 75% until it falls once.

•     Bearmarket: If the stock falls three times in a row the market is in a "Bearmarket" state. → The chances the sock falls again increases to 75% until it rises once.

•     Influencer: 60 seconds into the game Elons Musks starts tweeting about your stocks. Every second there is a 2% chance he tweets either positively or negatively.

        → A tweet manipulates the market for the following 6 rounds. Your stock is now rising/falling with a 80% chance depending on the tweet and the value chances increase to 4-8 points instead of 1-5.

[Profile]

What's that

The profile displays your user related data as well as your current total account balance and a diagram showing the split of "Cash" and "Stocks"-value in your account.

Below that you'll find you acitivity history, which shows you all the transactions you did since the beginning of the current game.

Sidenote: As long as you're at your profile-tab the actual game pauses. So if you want to take a break or rethink your strategy...

[Restart]

Resets the stock value, gametime, holding amount and account balance

[End Game]

Prematurly ends the game and shows you how you've done.

[Logout]

Logout and get redirected to the Login page. All game- and user-info will be deleted.
